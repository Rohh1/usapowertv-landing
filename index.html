  1. Get the Downloader app. From the Fire TV home screen, tap the Search icon (or press the microphone button on your remote) and search for "Downloader". Install it if you don't have it; otherwise launch it.
  2. Enter the download code. In Downloader, tap the URL field and enter code 785342 (or type xxlinked.me/goldclub), then press Go. Wait for the APK to finish downloading.
  3. Enable Unknown Sources (don't install yet). Press Home, then go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options. If "Developer Options" isn't visible, open About and click the device name 5 times until it says "You are now a developer." Back in Developer Options, choose Install Unknown Apps and toggle Downloader to On.
  4. Install the APK. Reopen Downloader, tap Install, and choose your player (TiviMate or GoldClub Purple). Tap Done or Open when the install finishes.
  5. Log in. Open the player (find it under Your Apps & Channels if needed). When prompted, enter the Username and Password from your welcome email.

Why does other IPTV buffer and USA Power TV doesn't?
Most cheap IPTV services oversell a single server to thousands of users — when everyone tunes in for a big game or PPV night, it crashes. We run 12 regional server zones with load balancing and automatic failover. If one region gets hot, we move you to another in seconds. If you're on the Power Box, the Yoka IPX2's Gigabit Ethernet + 4GB RAM eliminates the local hardware bottleneck too. End result: no spinning wheel during the 4th quarter.
What if I still get buffering?
99% of buffering we see is actually local WiFi (your router is too far, too many devices connected, or your ISP is throttling). Message us on WhatsApp — we'll test your connection, switch your server region, and fix it in minutes. If your setup genuinely can't support it, we'll refund the trial and point you to something else.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You buy in durations — 1, 3, 6, or 12 months. When it ends, it ends. No auto-renew unless you want it on.
